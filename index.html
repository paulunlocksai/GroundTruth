WELCOME

"Ground truth" is the data that AI models are trained on. It's also the perfect metaphor for what we're building here: cutting through the noise to get to what's real in AI.

WHY

AI is evolving at breakneck speed. To make sense of it, we need to cut through the noise and get signal from the people building the future. That's what Ground Truth delivers: collective wisdom from those on the front lines.

WHAT

A private monthly roundtable. Builders sharing knowledge and debating the most pressing issues in AI technology, business, UX, and ethics.

WHO

A carefully curated group of founders, execs, engineers, designers and PMs actively shaping AI-powered products and services. Because we believe that the best source of what's real isn't the headlines, it's the builders on the ground.

THE FORMAT

We discuss 2-3 timely topics at each session. The week prior members receive a briefing to get their thoughts flowing and prep for the discussion. Past topics have included "Measuring Productivity in an AI-First World" and "The LLM Performance Plateau." Each session also includes an "Insights" slot where members share what they've learned recently as builders. Members also have access to a private Slack channel to continue the discussion.

LOCATION

Los Angeles (virtual attendance coming soon)
